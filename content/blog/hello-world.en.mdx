Welcome to my blog! I'm Germán Sandoval, a Full-Stack Software Engineer based in Merida. This space is where I'll share what I'm learning, building, and thinking about in the world of software development.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Web development** — deep dives into Next.js, React, TypeScript, and the tools I use every day.
- **Architecture & patterns** — thoughts on clean code, scalable systems, and developer experience.
- **Career & growth** — lessons learned from shipping real products and working across teams.
- **Open source** — contributions, findings, and things that made me stop and say "that's clever."

## A Bit About Me

I've spent the last several years building web applications — from small SaaS products to large-scale platforms. I care deeply about writing code that's not just functional, but _maintainable_: the kind of codebase you can onboard a new engineer into without a week of tribal-knowledge sessions.

Outside of work, you'll find me experimenting with new tools, taking photos, or exploring Merida's food scene.
